Help Taos During the COVID-19 Outbreak by 3d Printing Valves That Could Help Save Lives

See How You Can Help

Do you have a 3D printer and want to help Taos during this COVID-19 outbreak? You can help increase Holy Cross Hospital’s ability to treat COVID-19 patients by printing adapter valves that allow a CPAP machine to be hooked up to full face snorkel mask to act like a ventilator. We are looking for volunteers with FDM 3D printers to print 50 total of these valves out of PLA plastic.

Have a Mask?

The design of the valve cannot be completed until the full face snorkel masks arrive in the mail, so if you have a U.S. Divers AirGo Full Face Snorkel System mask we could borrow for measurements, it could help speed up the process.

Inspiration

The idea for this project came from an Italian Company, Isinnova. They worked with local doctors to come up with the original design. We had to make some modifications to accommodate different snorkel masks and meet the requirements given by Holy Cross Hospital. You can check an article describing their work below.
